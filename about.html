About RK Education Dhorimnna

Established in 2008, RK Education Dhorimnna is a premier private secondary school dedicated to fostering academic excellence, character development, and holistic education. Located in the heart of Dhorimnna, our institution has been a beacon of learning, nurturing students to become confident, compassionate, and capable leaders of tomorrow.

Our commitment to a well-rounded education is reflected in our rigorous academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and a dedicated faculty with an average of 15 years of teaching experience. We strive to create an environment where intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and personal growth thrive.

Our History

2008 - Founding Year

RK Education Dhorimnna was established with a vision to provide quality education and foster holistic development in the region.

2020 - Academic Excellence
