About Veer Teja Public School

Founded in 2006, Veer Teja Public School has established itself as a premier private secondary school committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education.

Rigorous academic curriculum with Advanced courses

Dedicated faculty with an average of 15 years teaching experience

State-of-the-art facilities including STEM labs and performing arts center

"Veer Teja Public School has provided my child with not just an education, but a transformative experience that has shaped their character and prepared them for the challenges of university life."

— Poonmaram Ji, Parent

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students at every level.

STEM Program

Our Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics program prepares students for careers in these high-demand fields through hands-on learning and research opportunities.

Robotics Coding Engineering

Arts & Humanities

Our comprehensive arts program nurtures creativity through visual arts, music, theater, and creative writing, while our humanities curriculum explores history, philosophy, and literature.

Visual Arts Music Theater

International Baccalaureate

Our IB Diploma Programme is recognized worldwide as a premier preparation for university, emphasizing critical thinking, intercultural understanding, and rigorous academics.

IB Diploma Global Focus University Prep

Athletics

Our competitive sports program includes soccer, basketball, swimming, track & field, and more, emphasizing teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.

Soccer Basketball Swimming

Community Service

We believe in developing socially responsible citizens. Our service learning program connects classroom learning with meaningful community engagement.

Volunteering Leadership Social Impact

Technology Integration

Our 1:1 device program ensures students develop digital literacy skills while using technology as a tool for creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving.

Coding Digital Arts Media Production

Our World-Class Facilities

Designed to inspire learning, creativity, and personal growth.

Advanced Science Labs

Our fully-equipped laboratories for biology, chemistry, and physics allow students to conduct sophisticated experiments and research projects.

Learning Resource Center

Our modern library features over 25,000 print and digital resources, collaborative study spaces, and a dedicated research assistance program.

Athletic Complex

Includes a gymnasium, fitness center, outdoor track, soccer field, and tennis courts to support our comprehensive athletic program.

Performing Arts Center

A 500-seat theater with professional lighting and sound systems, plus dedicated spaces for music, dance, and drama rehearsals.

Dining Hall

Our nutritionist-designed meal program offers healthy, balanced options with accommodations for dietary restrictions.
