Our World-Class Facilities

Designed to inspire learning, creativity, and personal growth.

Advanced Science Labs

Our fully-equipped laboratories for biology, chemistry, and physics allow students to conduct sophisticated experiments and research projects.

Learning Resource Center

Our modern library features over 25,000 print and digital resources, collaborative study spaces, and a dedicated research assistance program.

Athletic Complex

Includes a gymnasium, fitness center, outdoor track, soccer field, and tennis courts to support our comprehensive athletic program.

Performing Arts Center

A 500-seat theater with professional lighting and sound systems, plus dedicated spaces for music, dance, and drama rehearsals.

Dining Hall

Our nutritionist-designed meal program offers healthy, balanced options with accommodations for dietary restrictions.
